Bas Boerboom

Composer & Musician

My name is Bas Boerboom!

From an early age, I began playing guitar and piano. Today, I work as a composer and producer for media. Blending my classical background with my love for guitar focused music, I write orchestral scores ranging from intimate and melancholic to epic and euphoric.
